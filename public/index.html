Frequently asked questions

What is a URL shortener?

A URL shortener is a tool that turns a long web address into a short, easy-to-share link. When someone opens the short link, they're instantly redirected to the original long URL.

How do I shorten a URL for free?

Paste your long URL into the box at the top of this page and click Shorten. You'll get a short link you can copy and share right away — no signup needed.

Can I create a custom short link?

Yes. When shortening, enter your own custom code (the "back-half") — 4 to 30 letters, numbers or dashes — to make a branded, memorable link like theurlshortner.com/my-sale.

Can I track how many people click my link?

Yes. Every short link counts its clicks. Create a free account to see all your links and their click counts in one dashboard.

Do short links expire?

No. Your short links stay active and keep redirecting to the original URL.

Is Shortly free to use?

Yes, Shortly is completely free to shorten URLs and track clicks.
