How to shorten a YouTube link

To shorten a YouTube link, copy the video or channel URL, paste it into TheURLShortner, and click Shorten. You’ll get a short, clean link — free of long tracking parameters — that’s easy to share in descriptions, comments, and bios, plus a QR code and click tracking.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

How do I shorten a YouTube link?

  1. On YouTube, click Share and copy the video or channel URL (or copy it from the address bar).
  2. Paste it on the TheURLShortner homepage.
  3. Optionally add a custom code like /my-video, then click Shorten.
  4. Copy your short link and paste it anywhere.

Why shorten YouTube links?

  • Clean links in video descriptions and social bios.
  • Track how many people clicked your video link.
  • Branded codes look more trustworthy than long YouTube URLs.
