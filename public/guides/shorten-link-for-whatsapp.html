How to shorten a link for WhatsApp

To shorten a link for WhatsApp, paste your long URL into TheURLShortner and click Shorten. The tidy short link is easy to tap and looks trustworthy in chats and status updates, instead of a long address that wraps awkwardly. It’s free, needs no signup, and tracks clicks.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

How do I shorten a link for WhatsApp?

  1. Copy the long link you want to send.
  2. Paste it on the TheURLShortner homepage.
  3. Optionally add a custom code, then click Shorten.
  4. Tap Copy and paste the short link into your WhatsApp chat or status.

Why use short links on WhatsApp?

  • Clean, tappable links that don't break across lines.
  • Look more trustworthy than long, parameter-filled URLs.
  • Track how many people opened the link you shared.
