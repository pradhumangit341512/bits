Free QR code generator for any link

Every link you shorten with TheURLShortner comes with a free QR code. Scan it with any phone camera to open the link — perfect for posters, packaging, business cards, and menus.

How to get a QR code for your link

  1. Paste your long URL on the homepage and click Shorten.
  2. Your short link appears with a QR code right below it.
  3. Click Download QR code to save the image (SVG — scales to any size without blurring).

Where QR codes are useful

  • Print: flyers, posters, packaging, business cards.
  • In person: restaurant menus, event signage, shop windows.
  • Tracking: because the QR points to your short link, you also see the click count.

Why SVG QR codes?

The QR is a vector (SVG), so it stays razor-sharp whether you use it on a tiny card or a large banner — no pixelation. Update the link's destination from your dashboard and the same printed QR keeps working.
