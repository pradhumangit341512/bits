URL shortener — no signup needed

Yes. TheURLShortner is a completely free URL shortener that needs no signup to shorten links. You can create unlimited short links, add custom codes, generate QR codes, and track clicks at no cost. A free account is optional and simply adds a dashboard to manage all your links.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

What's included for free?

  • Unlimited short links — no monthly cap to shorten URLs.
  • Custom back-half codes — brand your links at no cost.
  • Click tracking — see how many times each link is opened.
  • No signup needed to shorten; a free account adds a dashboard.

How do I shorten a link for free?

  1. Paste your long URL on the homepage.
  2. (Optional) type a custom code.
  3. Click Shorten and copy your free short link.
