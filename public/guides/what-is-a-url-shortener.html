What is a URL shortener?

A URL shortener is a tool that turns a long web address into a short, easy-to-share link. When someone clicks the short link, they're instantly redirected to the original long URL.

How does a short link work?

When you shorten a URL, the shortener stores your long link and gives it a unique short code — the part after the slash, like theurlshortner.com/my-link. Opening the short link sends a redirect to the original address, so visitors land exactly where you intended.

Why use a URL shortener?

  • Cleaner sharing: long links break across lines in chats, emails and texts. Short links stay clickable.
  • More trust & clicks: short, readable links look less spammy and get clicked more often.
  • Click tracking: you can see how many people opened your link.
  • Branding: custom codes make links memorable, like /summer-sale.

Are short links safe?

Short links are as safe as the destination they point to. With theurlshortner, every link simply redirects to the original URL you entered — nothing is changed about the destination.

How do I make a short link?

It takes one step: paste your long URL on the theurlshortner homepage and click Shorten. For a walkthrough, see how to shorten a URL, or learn to create a custom short link.
