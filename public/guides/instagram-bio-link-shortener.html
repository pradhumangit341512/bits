Link shortener for your Instagram bio

Instagram gives you one link in your bio, so it should be short, clean, and memorable. A custom short link looks professional and lets you see how many followers actually tap it.

Make your bio link

  1. Copy the destination you want to send followers to (shop, latest post, linktree, etc.).
  2. Paste it on the homepage.
  3. Add a custom code that matches your handle, like /yourname.
  4. Click Shorten and paste the link into your Instagram bio.

Tips for bio links

  • Use your brand or handle in the code so it's recognizable.
  • Sign in to your dashboard to update the destination later without changing your bio.
  • Track clicks to learn which posts drive the most profile taps.
