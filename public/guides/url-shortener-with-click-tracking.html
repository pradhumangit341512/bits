URL shortener with click tracking

Every link you shorten with TheURLShortner automatically counts its clicks — no analytics code or setup required. Just shorten your URL, share it, and the click total updates each time someone opens it. Create a free account to see every link and its click count together in one dashboard.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

How does click tracking work?

When someone opens your short link, TheURLShortner records the visit and redirects them to the original URL. Your click count updates in real time and is visible on the result card and in your dashboard.

How do I see all my link stats?

  1. Create a free account and open your dashboard.
  2. Every link shows its short URL, destination, and total clicks.
  3. Edit a link's destination any time without changing the short URL.

Why track clicks?

  • Know which posts, emails, or channels drive the most traffic.
  • Compare campaigns using different custom codes.
  • Prove engagement without complex analytics tools.

Click tracking FAQ

How do I see how many clicks my link has?

Create a free account and open your dashboard to see every link and its click count. You can also check a single link's stats any time.

Does click tracking cost anything?

No. Click tracking is included free on every short link you create.

Do I need to install anything to track clicks?

No. Tracking is automatic — there's no analytics code or plugin to install. Every short link counts its clicks by default.
