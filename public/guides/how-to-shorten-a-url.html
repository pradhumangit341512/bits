How to shorten a URL (free, step by step)

To shorten a URL for free, paste your long link into the box on the TheURLShortner homepage, optionally add a custom code, and click Shorten. You'll instantly get a short link you can copy, share, or turn into a QR code — no signup or app required.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

How do I shorten a URL step by step?

  1. Paste your long URL. Copy the web address you want to share and paste it into the input box on the TheURLShortner homepage. Any valid http:// or https:// link works.
  2. (Optional) choose a custom code. Type a custom back-half — 4 to 30 letters, numbers or dashes — so your link reads like theurlshortner.com/my-sale, or leave it blank for a random 6-character code. See custom short links for tips.
  3. Click Shorten and copy. Your short link appears instantly with a one-click Copy button. Paste it anywhere — social posts, messages, emails, or print.

How do I track my clicks?

Create a free account to open your dashboard, where every link shows its click count and you can edit or delete links any time.
