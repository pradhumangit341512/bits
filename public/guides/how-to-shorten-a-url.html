How to shorten a URL (free, step by step)

Shortening a URL takes less than a minute and is completely free on theurlshortner — no account needed. Here's exactly how.

Step 1: Paste your long URL

Copy the long web address you want to share, then paste it into the input box on the theurlshortner homepage. Any valid http:// or https:// link works.

Step 2: (Optional) choose a custom code

Want a branded link? Type a custom back-half — 4 to 30 letters, numbers or dashes — so your link reads like theurlshortner.com/my-sale. Leave it blank to get a random 6-character code. See custom short links for tips.

Step 3: Click Shorten and copy

Click Shorten. Your short link appears instantly with a one-click Copy button. Paste it anywhere — social posts, messages, emails, or print.

Track your clicks (optional)

Create a free account to open your dashboard, where every link shows its click count and you can edit or delete links any time.
